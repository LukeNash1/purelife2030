Are You Awake? — Prophetic Countdown

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Are You Awake? — Prophetic Countdown Chrome extension and the associated webpage, created by C. Luke Humphreys.

What Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. The extension does not track you, identify you, or transmit any information about you to any server.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage permission solely to cache the current day count on your own device. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Alarms

The extension uses Chrome's alarms permission to refresh the badge count once per day. This is a purely local operation — no network request is made.

Network Requests

The extension popup loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to display correctly. This is a standard web font request and is subject to Google's own privacy policy. No other network requests are made by the extension.

Email Capture (Website Only)

The prophetic timeline webpage includes an optional email signup form. If you choose to submit your email address, it is collected solely for the purpose of sending ministry updates from C. Luke Humphreys. Your email is never sold, shared, or used for any other purpose. You may unsubscribe at any time.

Third-Party Links

The extension and webpage contain links to YouTube and Amazon. These are standard external links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms.

Children

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given that no data is collected, changes are unlikely.
